Room Finder

Abstract

Science and Technology has changed this world into a small, secure and easy to manage every activity of the individual. Thus, the use of the Room Rental management system is needed to search the available rooms/houses for rent in a city at our fingertips.

In order to give easy access to find rooms for students and employees as per their need, this Room Finder application was developed which provides a platform for house owners who have House/Flat/Rooms for rent and people who need them.

This application will be able to show the available rooms/houses and contact details of their owners within a particular area and cost selected by the user.
